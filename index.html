About Us

At Asli Bar,

We create unforgettable nights where culture, entertainment, and luxury collide. Whether you're relaxing at our exclusive VIP tables, or enjoying our signature Shisha & Drinks, every moment is crafted for your pleasure. Our bar comes alive with themed events such as Arabic Nights, Bollywood Nights, African Nights, and Persian Nights, bringing vibrant energy and unique experiences to each evening. With world-class DJs, live entertainment, and a welcoming ambiance, Asli Bar is the perfect place to unwind, celebrate, or simply indulge. Here, diversity in music, flavor, and service come together to create a night you’ll never forget.

Our Menu

At Asli Bar is designed to satisfy every craving, from refreshing drinks to savory bites.
